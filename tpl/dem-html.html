ALIGNMENTS

After this horizontal rule (<HR>) a new line is now started with <BR>.
A new paragraph is now started with <P> which skips a line.

A paragraph can be aligned to the right with <P align=right>.
Then all text is aligned to the right.

A paragraph can be aligned in the center with <P align=center>.
Then all text is aligned in the center.

Centered text is placed between <CENTER> and </CENTER>

Text can be aligned at the right side of the page. This is done with <P align=justify>. You can break words (<WBR>) or prevent this to happen (<NOBR>).

FIGURES

Figures can be included in the page. Its recommended to include the 'alt'-tag.

<IMG src=".." width=100 height=80>

<IMG src=".." alt="text">

<IMG src=".." alt="text" border="5">

<IMG src=".." align=left> picture left, text right

<IMG src=".." align=right> picture right, text left

<IMG src=".." align=left> picture left,text right
<BR clear=left> (|right|all) text under picture
